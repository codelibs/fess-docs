Versioning Policy
=================

Versions are provided in the format X.Y.Z.

-  X: Major version. Incremented when Fess's Elasticsearch
   schema is updated.

-  Y: Minor version. Incremented for feature additions or bug fixes
   that do not update Fess's Elasticsearch schema.

-  Z:
   Micro version. Incremented for critical bug fixes.
   (May also be incremented for non-critical fixes based on user requests, handled case by case)
